This is a blog about cats

Cats are one of the animals that many people, including myself, love as pets. This is mostly because they are adorable, loving and such playful > animals. They are also very clean.

Other reasons according to goodhousekeeping is that:

  1. They can help kids learn better

    Cats have a lot of positive effects on children. They can help them learn social skills, cope with traumatic events and make them less likely to have allergies.

  2. They are the ideal pets for apartments

    Unlike dogs, cat are very quiet and hence very suitable for those living in apartments as there is less disturbance to your neighbours.

  3. They have need less care than dogs

    When it comes to food, entertainment and grooming , cats are much cheaper than dogs.
